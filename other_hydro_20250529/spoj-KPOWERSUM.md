<p>Task Is: You Will Be Given A Number(N) And Another Number(K). <strong>Now You Have To Find &nbsp;K<sub>th</sub> Power Summation Of Divisors Of N.</strong></p>

<p>Summation Of All Divisors Of <strong>N</strong> Will Be Huge, &nbsp;So You Have To Print The Summation Module (M=1000000007).</p>
<p><strong>Like: Divisors Of 6 is: ( 1 2 3 6 ) And K = 2.<br> so, summation is: 1^K+2^K+3^K+6^K = 1^2 + 2^2 + 3^2 + 6^2= 1+4+9+36 = 50%1000000007=50</strong></p>
