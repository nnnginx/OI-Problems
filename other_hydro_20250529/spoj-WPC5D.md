<p><span style="font-size: small;">There are k galaxies that participate in the Galactical Wars, which have happened for the past n years. Each year there has been a victor. Further, a particular galaxy G has won the Wars an <strong>even</strong> number of times. Also, G <strong>didn't</strong> participate in the first year.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: small;">You are a member of the old and wise community of Daba. Even though it is the festive season of Galaxy going on, you prefer to sit in your room and do complicated (and senseless) calculations. For no reason, one day, you wonder in how many possible ways the victories in the previous years could have taken place (that is, the number of different sequences of victors possible). Output the result of this complicated (and senseless) calculation.&nbsp;</span></p>
