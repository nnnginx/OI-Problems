<p>You are currently studying the language <strong><em>Ajob</em></strong> ( which means strange in English ) from a renowned professor. The language has infinite number of letters in its alphabet ( now you know, why it is called ajob ). &nbsp;<br><br>The professor taught you <strong><em>N</em></strong> words, one by one. The number of letters in a word is equal to it's place in the teaching order. Thus, the <em><strong>1<sup>st</sup></strong></em> word taught by the professor has <em><strong>1</strong></em> letter, <em><strong>2<sup>nd</sup></strong></em> word has <em><strong>2</strong></em> letters, <em><strong>3<sup>rd</sup></strong></em> word has <em><strong>3</strong></em> letters, ..., the <em><strong>N<sup>th</sup></strong></em> word has <em><strong>N</strong></em> letters. &nbsp;<br><br><em>All the letters within a word are distinct to each other</em>.<br><br>Now, you are handed an assignment. You have to choose any one of the <em><strong>N</strong></em> words and form a subsequence from it. The length of the subsequence should be <strong>exactly</strong> <em><strong>K</strong></em> less than the length of original word. For example, if the length of the chosen word is <em><strong>L</strong></em>, then the length of the subsequence should be <em><strong>L-K</strong></em>. If for any word, <em><strong>L</strong></em> is smalller than <em><strong>K</strong></em> <em><strong>(L&lt;K)</strong></em>, then you must not choose that word. Two subsequences are different to each other if, the lengths of them are different or they contain different characters in the same position.<br><br>Find the number of ways you can complete the assignment<em><strong> modulo p</strong></em> ( <em><strong>p</strong></em> will always be a <em>prime</em> ).</p>
<h3>Input</h3>
<p>The first line contains <em><strong>T</strong></em>, the number of testcases. The next <em><strong>T</strong></em> lines contain three space separated integers <em><strong>N</strong></em>, <em><strong>K</strong></em> and <em><strong>p</strong></em>.</p>
<h3>Output</h3>
<p>For each testcase, print one integer in a single line, the number possible ways you can complete the assignment, <em><strong>modulo p</strong></em>.</p>
