<h3>Input</h3>
<p>the first line starts with a number, T, wich is the number of test cases, T lines will follow</p>
<p>each line contains a number "n" that is the nth term of the sum from 2^0 to 2^n</p>
<p>0&lt;=n&lt;=500</p>
<h3>Output</h3>
<p>Output the sum from 2^0 to 2^n MODULO <span>1298074214633706835075030044377087</span></p>
