<p>Leo is training his martial arts in byteland's dojo.<br> The dojo is a rectangular ( n ¡Á m ) area with a pit of death, a square located at (i, j), where 1 &lt;= i &lt;= n, and 1 &lt;= j &lt;= m.<br>Every night, battles are very hard and the dojo is destructed, and reconstructed during the day around the pit.<br>Every evening, Leo's first mission is to make in place tatamis ( 2 ¡Á 1 ) in the new dojo.<br>Tatamis should cover the whole dojo except the pit of death.<br>Sometimes it's possible, sometimes not!<br><br><br></p>
<h3>Input</h3>
<p>The input begins with the number T of test cases in a single line.<br>In each of the next T lines there are four integers n, m, i and j.</p>
<h3>Output</h3>
<p>For each test case, print the answer of the problem : "Possible." or "Impossible.".</p>
<h3>Example</h3>
<pre><strong>Input:</strong>
2<br>3 3 2 2<br>3 3 1 2<br>

<strong>Output:</strong>
Possible.<br>Impossible.<br>
</pre>
<h3>Constraints</h3>
<p>1 &lt;= T &lt;= 1 000<br>1 &lt;= i &lt;= n &lt;= 32<br>1 &lt;= j &lt;= m &lt;= 10<sup>2000</sup></p>
