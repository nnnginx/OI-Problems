<p><span style="font-family: Times-Roman; font-size: 11pt;">A university gives scholarship to students based on their current CGPA. This waiver allocation<br><span style="font-size: 11pt;">follows some rules. At the beginning of a semester allotted scholarships for certain CGPA range is<br><span style="font-size: 11pt;">put on the notice board in tabular form. Two such tables are shown below:<br><span style="font-size: 11pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt;"><span style="font-size: 11pt;"><img src="file://jXDvL6l1.png" alt="" width="600" height="116"><br><span style="font-size: 11pt;">In the table on the left it is said that students who have CGPA within 3.60 and 3.69 (inclusive) will<br><span style="font-size: 11pt;">get 10% of their tuition fee as scholarship. Similarly, those with CGPA range is within 3.90-3.99<br><span style="font-size: 11pt;">will get 40% of their tuition fee as scholarship. Using the two tables above we will try to explain to<br><span style="font-size: 11pt;">you the rules and restrictions of allocating waiver:<br><span style="font-size: 11pt;">1. A fixed Scholarship is given to all the students within a CGPA Range.<br><span style="font-size: 11pt;">2. The range of each CGPA slab must be equal (Except the topmost one, which can be smaller).<br><span style="font-size: 11pt;">The slabs must go all the way up to 4.00.<br><span style="font-size: 11pt;">3. The scholarship percentage for the lowest slab is a positive integer. With the increase of<br><span style="font-size: 11pt;">CGPA range the amount of scholarship percentage must also increase by a fixed positive<br><span style="font-size: 11pt;">integer value. In the table on the left this fixed value is 10% and in the table on the right this<br><span style="font-size: 11pt;">fixed value is 30%.<br><span style="font-size: 11pt;">4. Scholarship percentage for each slab is always a positive integer with a maximum value of<br><span style="font-size: 11pt;">100%.<br><span style="font-size: 11pt;">5. All the scholarships given should use up a given amount, P units.<br><span style="font-size: 11pt;">7. A CGPA range for scholarship cannot start below 2.50. So 2.50-2.55 is a valid CGPA range<br><span style="font-size: 11pt;">but 2.45-2.55 is not valid.<br><span style="font-size: 11pt;">8. There must be at least 2 slabs for scholarship. But of course a slab can contain zero students.</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span><br style="orphans: 2; text-align: -webkit-auto; widows: 2;"></span><span style="font-family: Times-Roman; font-size: 11pt;">Given the number of students, total available scholarship units and their current CGPA, your job is<br><span style="font-size: 11pt;">to find out the number of different possible scholarship allocations that uses up all the scholarship<br><span style="font-size: 11pt;">units. Two scholarship allocations are different if their CGPA range is different or scholarship<br><span style="font-size: 11pt;">allocation in any slab is different.<br><span style="font-family: Times-Bold; font-size: 11pt;"><strong>Input</strong><br><span style="font-family: Times-Roman; font-size: 11pt;">Input file contains at most 100 sets of inputs. The description of each set is given below:<br><span style="font-size: 11pt;">Each set starts with two integers <span style="font-family: Times-Italic; font-size: 11pt;"><em>N </em><span style="font-family: Times-Roman; font-size: 11pt;">(N&lt;=10000) which indicates the total number of students and <span style="font-family: Times-Italic; font-size: 11pt;"><em>P</em><br><span style="font-family: Times-Roman; font-size: 11pt;">which indicates the scholarship units that needs to be used up. Each of the next <span style="font-family: Times-Italic; font-size: 11pt;"><em>N </em><span style="font-family: Times-Roman; font-size: 11pt;">lines contains the<br><span style="font-size: 11pt;">CGPA of one student.<br><span style="font-size: 11pt;">Input is terminated by a line containing two zeroes.</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
