<pre><br><span style="white-space: normal;"><p style="margin-bottom: 0in; text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><strong>India in Box</strong></span></span></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in; text-align: center;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in;" align="JUSTIFY"><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"> There is a well-known challenge in India. The challenge is as follows:</span></span></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in;" align="JUSTIFY">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in;" align="JUSTIFY"><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"> A merchant had some boxes. The boxes had weight and cost. He wants to give K boxes to one guy and the rest to other guy. Each guy has an average that is calculated by taking (Sum of Weights) / (Sum of Costs) of this guy. He wants to know what the smallest possible sum of the averages of the two guys he can achieve by making the distribution of the boxes.</span></span></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in;" align="JUSTIFY">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in;" align="JUSTIFY"><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"> Your task is to solve the challenge.</span></span></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in;" align="JUSTIFY">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in; text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><strong>Input</strong></span></span></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in;" align="CENTER">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in;" align="JUSTIFY"><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"> The input contains several test cases. A test case begins with a line containing two integers N and K (1 &lt;= K &lt; N &lt;= 100) representing the total number of boxes and the value of K, respectively. The next N lines contains two integers each, wi and ci (1 &lt;= wi, ci &lt;= 50), indicating the weight and cost of the ith box, respectively. </span></span></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in;" align="JUSTIFY"><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"> The last test case is followed by a line containing a single 0.</span></span></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in;" align="JUSTIFY">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in; text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><strong>Output</strong></span></span></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in; text-align: center;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in;" align="JUSTIFY"><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: small;"> For each test case, print a line containing a single value, the smallest possible sum of the averages, accurate to 5 decimal places.</span></span></p>
