<p><span style="font-size: small;">There is a giant circular container which is divided into N segments numbered from 0 to N-1.&nbsp;<br><br>Each segment has two bottles, one is a bottle of cold-drink and other is a bottle of poision. Drinking from bottle of cold-drink increases your energy by A[i] while drinking from bottle of poision decreases your energy by B[i].<br><br>The corresponding energies for the same are given in input.<br><br>Now as it is circular, so bottles in segment N-1 and segment 0 are adjacent. If you do not drink from bottle of cold-drink, then you have to drink from bottle of poision in that segment. Furthermore you cannot drink from bottle of cold-drink in adjacent segments. You have to drink in such a way so that your energy maximizes. Find this maximum value.</span></p>
