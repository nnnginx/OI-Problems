<p>The sequence of Tetranacci numbers is defined as follows: <br> a<sub>n</sub> = a<sub>n-1</sub> + a<sub>n-2</sub> + a<sub>n-3</sub> + a<sub>n-4</sub> with   a<sub>0</sub> = a<sub>1</sub> = a<sub>2</sub> = 0 and a<sub>3</sub> = 1.</p>
<h3>Input</h3>
<p>Input starts with a positive integer t ¡Ü 4000, then t lines follow.  Each of the t lines contains two space separated integers m and n with 0 ¡Ü m ¡Ü n ¡Ü 10<sup>9</sup>.</p>
<h3>Output</h3>
<p>Calculate a<sub>m</sub> + a<sub>m+1</sub> + ... + a<sub>n</sub> and print the result modulo 1000000007.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>Example</h3>
<pre><strong>Input:</strong>
2
1 2
1919 5393

<strong>Output:</strong>
0
66616
</pre>
