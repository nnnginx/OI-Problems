<p>The problem is very simple. given two integers n and m, find the product GCD(1, 1) * GCD(1, 2) * ... * GCD(1, M) * GCD(2, 1) * GCD(2, 2) * ... * GCD(2, M) * ... * GCD(N, 1) * GCD(N, 2) * ... * GCD(N, M).</p>
<h3>Input</h3>
<p>The first line will be the number of test cases t, followed by t lines , each having two numbers n and m (1 &lt;= n, m &lt;= 10000000) (1 &lt;= t &lt;= 5).</p>
<h3>Output</h3>
<p>Output the required solution modulo 10^9+7.</p>
<h3>Example</h3>
<pre><strong>Input:</strong>
1
5 6

<strong>Output:</strong>
5760</pre>
