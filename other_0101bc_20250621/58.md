<h2>说明</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输入一个小写字母，则变为大写输出。</span>
<h2>输入格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输入为一个小写字符。</span>
<h2>输出格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">对应的大写字母。</span>
<h2>样例</h2>
<pre><code class="language-input1">a</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">A</code></pre>
