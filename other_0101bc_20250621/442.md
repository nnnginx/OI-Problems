<h2>说明</h2>


	<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">给出一个正整数</span>a<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">，要求分解成若干个正整数的乘积，即</span>a=a1×a2×a3×...×an<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">，并且</span>1<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&lt;</span>a1<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">≤</span>a2<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">≤</span>a3<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">≤ ...≤</span>an<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">，问这样的分解的方案种数有多少。注意到</span>a=a<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">也是―种分解。</span>
<br />

	<br />
<br />
<h2>输入格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">第1行是测试数据的组数</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">(</span>1≤n≤10<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">)，后面跟着</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">行输入。每组测试数据占</span>1<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">行，包括―个正整数</span>a<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">(</span>1&lt;a&lt;32768<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">)。</span>
<h2>输出格式</h2>

n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">行，每行输出对应一个输入。输出应是一个正整数，指明满足要求的分解的方案种数。</span>
<h2>样例</h2>
<pre><code class="language-input1">2
2
20</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">1
4</code></pre>
