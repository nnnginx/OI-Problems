<h2>说明</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">A同学认为自己的幸运数是不超过</span>N<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">的非负整数里能被</span>K<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">整除的最大的数，你能帮他的算幸运数吗?</span>
<h2>输入格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输入―行，包含两个整数</span>N<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&#44;</span>K<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">(</span>1≤N&#44;K≤2×10<sup>9</sup><span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;"><sup></sup>)。</span>
<h2>输出格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输出―行，包含一个整数，表示幸运数。</span>
<h2>样例</h2>
<pre><code class="language-input1">3 2</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">2</code></pre>
