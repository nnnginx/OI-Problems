<h2>说明</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输出正整数a到b之间的所有素数。</span>

<h2>输入格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输入</span>a<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&#44;</span>b<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">。对于所有数据，</span>1≤a≤b≤2000000<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">。</span>

<h2>输出格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">由小到大，输出</span>a<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">到</span>b<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">之间的所有素数。每个数占一行。</span>

<h2>样例</h2>
<pre><code class="language-input1">5 10</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">5
7</code></pre>
