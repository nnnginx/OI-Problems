<h2>说明</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">某医院想统计一下某项疾病的获得与否与年龄是否有关，需要对以前的诊断记录进行整理，按照&nbsp;</span>0−18<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">、</span>19−35<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">、</span>36−60<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">、</span>61<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">以上（含&nbsp;</span>61<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">）四个年龄段统计的患病人数占总患病人数的比例。</span>
<h2>输入格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">共&nbsp;</span>2<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">行，第一行为过往病人的数目&nbsp;</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">(</span>0&lt;n≤10<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">)，第二行为每个病人患病时的年龄，年龄为不超过&nbsp;</span>100<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;的非负整数。</span>
<h2>输出格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">照&nbsp;</span>0−18<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">、</span>19−35<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">、</span>36−60<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">、</span>61<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;以上（含&nbsp;</span>61<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">）四个年龄段输出该段患病人数占总患病人数的比例，以百分比的形式输出，精确到小数点后两位。每个年龄段占一行，共四行。</span>
