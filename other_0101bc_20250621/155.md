<h2>说明</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输入一个正整数&nbsp;</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">，找到</span>1<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">到</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">里最大的质数&#44;如果没有质数则输出"</span>No<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">"。</span>
<h2>输入格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输入一个正整数&nbsp;</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">(</span>n≤2×10^7<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">)。</span>
<h2>输出格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输出&nbsp;</span>1<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">到</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">里最大的质数，如果没有质数，则输出"</span>No<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">"。输出占一行。</span>
