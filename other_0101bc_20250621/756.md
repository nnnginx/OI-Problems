<h2>说明</h2>

<p class="p1" style="font-size:14px;font-family:&quot;">
	幼儿园中班有<span class="s1" style="line-height:normal;font-family:&quot;"> 36 </span>个小朋友，小班有<span class="s1" style="line-height:normal;font-family:&quot;"> 30 </span>个小朋友。按班分组，两个班各组的人数一样多，问每组最多有多少个小朋友<span class="s1" style="line-height:normal;font-family:&quot;">?</span> 
</p>
<h2>输入格式</h2>

输入x&#44;y，x和y的值分别是36和30
<h2>输出格式</h2>

每组最多几个小朋友？
<h2>样例</h2>
<pre><code class="language-input1">30 36</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">6</code></pre>
