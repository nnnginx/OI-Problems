<h2>说明</h2>

输入一个正整数n，<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">生成一个层数为</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">的金字塔。详细图形请看样例。注意</span>n=1<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">时就没有地板了。</span>
<h2>输入格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">第一行一个整数</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">(</span>1≤n≤100<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">)，表示层数。</span>
<h2>输出格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输出</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">层金字塔，注意金字塔是完全对称的，在金字塔的右侧也需要输出与左侧相同的空格。</span>
<h2>样例</h2>
<pre><code class="language-input1">5</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">    /\
   /__\
  /____\
 /______\
/________\</code></pre>
