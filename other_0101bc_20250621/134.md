<h2>说明</h2>

脱氧核糖核酸（DNA）由两条互补的碱基链以双螺旋的方式结合而成。而构成 DNA 的碱基共有<span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-1-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-1"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-2"><span class="mn" id="MathJax-Span-3" style="font-family:MathJax_Main;">4</span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.063em;"></span></span></span>种，分别为腺瞟呤（<span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-2-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-4"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-5"><span class="mi" id="MathJax-Span-6" style="font-family:MathJax_Math;font-style:italic;">A</span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.063em;"></span></span></span>）、鸟嘌呤（<span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-3-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-7"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-8"><span class="mi" id="MathJax-Span-9" style="font-family:MathJax_Math;font-style:italic;">G</span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.09em;"></span></span></span>）、胸腺嘧啶（<span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-4-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-10"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-11"><span class="mi" id="MathJax-Span-12" style="font-family:MathJax_Math;font-style:italic;">T<span></span></span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.063em;"></span></span></span>）和胞嘧啶（<span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-5-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-13"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-14"><span class="mi" id="MathJax-Span-15" style="font-family:MathJax_Math;font-style:italic;">C<span></span></span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.09em;"></span></span></span>）。我们知道，在两条互补碱基链的对应位置上，腺瞟呤总是和胸腺嘧啶配对，鸟嘌呤总是和胞嘧啶配对。你的任务就是根据一条单链上的碱基序列，给出对应的互补链上的碱基序列。
<h2>输入格式</h2>

一个字符串，表示一条碱基链。这个字符串只含有大写字母 <span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-6-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-16"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-17"><span class="mi" id="MathJax-Span-18" style="font-family:MathJax_Math;font-style:italic;">A</span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.063em;"></span></span></span>、<span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-7-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-19"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-20"><span class="mi" id="MathJax-Span-21" style="font-family:MathJax_Math;font-style:italic;">T<span></span></span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.063em;"></span></span></span>、<span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-8-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-22"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-23"><span class="mi" id="MathJax-Span-24" style="font-family:MathJax_Math;font-style:italic;">G</span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.09em;"></span></span></span>、<span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-9-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-25"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-26"><span class="mi" id="MathJax-Span-27" style="font-family:MathJax_Math;font-style:italic;">C<span></span></span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.09em;"></span></span></span>，分别表示腺瞟呤、胸腺嘧啶、鸟嘌呤和胞嘧啶。字符串长度不超过 <span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-10-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-28"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-29"><span class="mn" id="MathJax-Span-30" style="font-family:MathJax_Main;">255</span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.09em;"></span></span></span>。
<h2>输出格式</h2>

一个只含有大写字母 <span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-11-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-31"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-32"><span class="mi" id="MathJax-Span-33" style="font-family:MathJax_Math;font-style:italic;">A</span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.063em;"></span></span></span>、<span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-12-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-34"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-35"><span class="mi" id="MathJax-Span-36" style="font-family:MathJax_Math;font-style:italic;">T<span></span></span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.063em;"></span></span></span>、<span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-13-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-37"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-38"><span class="mi" id="MathJax-Span-39" style="font-family:MathJax_Math;font-style:italic;">G</span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.09em;"></span></span></span>、<span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-14-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-40"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-41"><span class="mi" id="MathJax-Span-42" style="font-family:MathJax_Math;font-style:italic;">C<span></span></span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.09em;"></span></span></span> 的字符串，为与输入的碱基链互补的碱基链。
