<h2>说明</h2>

<p class="p1" style="font-size:14px;font-family:&quot;">
	输人一个浮点数，输出其小数的位数。如输入<span class="s1" style="line-height:normal;font-family:&quot;"> 1.6</span>，输出<span class="s1" style="line-height:normal;font-family:&quot;">1；</span>输入<span class="s1" style="line-height:normal;font-family:&quot;">90.1234567890987654321</span>，输出<span class="s1" style="line-height:normal;font-family:&quot;"> 19</span>。<span class="s1" style="line-height:normal;font-family:&quot;">(</span>字符输入函数<span class="s1" style="line-height:normal;font-family:&quot;"> getchar()) </span>的作用是从终端输入一个字符，字符输出函数<span class="s1" style="line-height:normal;font-family:&quot;"> putchar()&nbsp;</span>的作用是向终端输出一个字符。<span class="s1" style="line-height:normal;font-family:&quot;">)</span> 
</p>
<h2>输入格式</h2>

一个字符串

<h2>输出格式</h2>

小数点后的位数

<h2>样例</h2>
<pre><code class="language-input1">90.1234567890987654321</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">19</code></pre>
