<h2>说明</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">根据参数，画出一个空心或实心的矩形。</span>
<h2>输入格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输入一行，包括四个参数：前两个参数为整数，依次代表矩形的高和宽（高不少于&nbsp;</span>3<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;行不多于&nbsp;</span>10<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;行，宽不少于&nbsp;</span>5<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;列不多于&nbsp;</span>10<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;列）；第三个参数是一个字符，表示用来画图的矩形符号；第四个参数为&nbsp;</span>1<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">或&nbsp;</span>0<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">，</span>0<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;代表空心，</span>1<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;代表实心。</span>
<h2>输出格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输出画出的图形。</span>
<h2>样例</h2>
<pre><code class="language-input1">7 7 @ 0</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">@@@@@@@
@     @
@     @
@     @
@     @
@     @
@@@@@@@</code></pre>
