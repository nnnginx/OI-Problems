<h2>说明</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">统计给定的&nbsp;</span>n(n &lt; 10<span style="font-size:10px;">000</span>)<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">个数中，负数、零和正数的个数。</span>
<h2>输入格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输入的第一个数是整数&nbsp;</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">，表示需要统计的数值的个数，然后是&nbsp;</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;个整数，-</span>100<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">≤ 所有数字 ≤</span>100<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">。</span>
<h2>输出格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输出一行&nbsp;</span>a<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&#44;</span>b<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;和&nbsp;</span>c<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">，分别表示给定的数据中负数、零和正数的个数。</span>
<h2>样例</h2>
