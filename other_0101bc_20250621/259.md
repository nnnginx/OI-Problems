<h2>说明</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">两个整数</span>a<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">和</span>b<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">分别作为分子和分母，即分数</span>a/b<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;，求它的浮点数值(双精度浮点数，保留小数点后</span>9<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">位)。</span>
<h2>输入格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输入仅一行，包括两个整数</span>a<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">和</span>b<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">。</span>
<h2>输出格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输出也仅一行，分数</span>a/b<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">的浮点数值（双精度浮点数，保留小数点后</span>9<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">位）。</span>
<h2>样例</h2>
<pre><code class="language-input1">5 7</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">0.714285714</code></pre>
