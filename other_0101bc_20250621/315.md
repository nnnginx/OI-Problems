<h2>说明</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">在国外，每月的</span>13<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">号和每周的星期5都是不吉利的。特别是当</span>13<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">号那天恰好是星期</span>5<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">时，更不吉利。已知某年的一月一日是星期</span>w<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">，并且这一年一定不是闰年，求出这一年所有</span>13<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">号那天是星期</span>5<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">的月份，按从小到大的顺序输出月份数字。(</span>w=1..7<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">)</span>
<h2>输入格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输入有一行，即一月一日星期几(</span>w<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">)。</span>
<h2>输出格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输出有―到多行，每行一个月份，表示该月的13日是星期五。</span>

<pre><code class="language-input1">7</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">1
10</code></pre>
