<h2>说明</h2>

<p style="text-indent:2em;font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;">
	牛牛最近学习了 C++入门课程，这门课程的总成绩计算方法是：
</p>
<p style="text-indent:2em;font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;">
	总成绩 = 作业成绩×2<span class="MJX_Assistive_MathML" style="vertical-align:0px;line-height:normal;">0</span>%+小测成绩×30%+期末考试成绩×5<span class="MJX_Assistive_MathML" style="vertical-align:0px;line-height:normal;">0</span>%
</p>
<p style="text-indent:2em;font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;">
	牛牛想知道，这门课程自己最终能得到多少分。
</p>
<h2>输入格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">只有 1 行，包含三个非负整数</span>A<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">、</span>B<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">、</span>C<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">，分别表示牛牛的作业成绩、小测成绩和期末考试成绩。相邻两个数之间用一个空格隔开，三项成绩满分都是&nbsp;</span>100<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;分。</span>0≤A、B、C≤100<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;且&nbsp;</span>A<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">、</span>B<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">、</span>C<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;都是&nbsp;</span>10<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;的整数倍。</span>
<h2>输出格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">只有 1 行，包含一个整数，即牛牛这门课程的总成绩，满分也是</span>100<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;分。</span>
<h2>样例</h2>
<pre><code class="language-input1">100 100 80</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">90</code></pre>
