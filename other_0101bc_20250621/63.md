<h2>说明</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">把一堆苹果分给</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">个小朋友，要使每个人都能拿到苹果，而且每个人拿到的苹果数都不同的话，这堆苹果至少应该有多少个？</span>
<h2>输入格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">一个不大于</span>1000<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">的正整数</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">，代表小朋友人数。</span>
<h2>输出格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">一个整数，表示满足条件的最少苹果个数。</span>
<h2>样例</h2>
<pre><code class="language-input1">8</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">36</code></pre>
