<h2>说明</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">给你一个整数，要求从个位开始分离出它的每一位数字。</span> <br />
<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;"><br />
</span> <br />
<h2>输入格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输入一个整数，整数在&nbsp;</span>1<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;到&nbsp;</span>10<sup>8</sup><span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp; 之间。</span>
<h2>输出格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">从个位开始按照从低位到高位的顺序依次输出每一位数字。数字之间以一个空格分开。</span>
<h2>样例</h2>
<pre><code class="language-input1">123
</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">3 2 1
</code></pre>
