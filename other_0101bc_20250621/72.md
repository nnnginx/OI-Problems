<h2>说明</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">在最近进行的考试中，小明考了&nbsp;</span>a<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">分，小科考了&nbsp;</span>b<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">分，那他们两个的分差（大减小）是多少呢。</span>
<h2>输入格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输入一行，包含两个整数&nbsp;</span>a<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&#44;&nbsp;</span>b<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">。对于</span>100<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">%的数据</span>1≤a，b≤10<sup>9</sup><span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">。</span>
<h2>输出格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输出一行，包含一个整数，表示答案。</span>
<h2>样例</h2>
<pre><code class="language-input1">1 3</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">2</code></pre>
