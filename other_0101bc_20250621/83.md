<h2>说明</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">我国现有&nbsp;</span>x<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">亿人口，如果按照每年&nbsp;</span>0.1%<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;的增长速度，</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;年后将有多少人？</span>
<h2>输入格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">一行，包含两个整数&nbsp;</span>x<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;和&nbsp;</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">，分别是人口基数和年数，以单个空格分隔。</span>1≤x≤100<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&#44;</span>1≤n≤100<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">。</span>
<h2>输出格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输出最后的人口数，以亿为单位，保留到小数点后四位。</span>
<h2>样例</h2>
<pre><code class="language-input1">13 10</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">13.1306</code></pre>
