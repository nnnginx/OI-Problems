<h2>说明</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">给出&nbsp;</span>10<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;个整数，问这些整数&nbsp;</span>(mod 42)&nbsp;<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">后有多少个不同的余数。</span>
<h2>输入格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输入共&nbsp;</span>10<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;行，每行一个不超过&nbsp;</span>10<sup>9</sup><span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;的正整数。</span>
<h2>输出格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">一个整数，表示不同的余数个数。</span>
<h2>样例</h2>
<pre><code class="language-input1">39
40
41
42
43
44
82
83
84
85</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">6</code></pre>

<h2>提示</h2>

<p style="text-indent:2em;font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;">
	【样例说明】
</p>
<pre>39 mod 42 = 39
40 mod 42 = 40
41 mod 42 = 41
42 mod 42 = 0
43 mod 42 = 1
44 mod 42 = 2
82 mod 42 = 40
83 mod 42 = 41
84 mod 42 = 0
85 mod 42 = 1</pre>
