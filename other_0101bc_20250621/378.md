<h2>说明</h2>

用手机发短信，一条短信资费为 <span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-1-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-1"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-2"><span class="mn" id="MathJax-Span-3" style="font-family:MathJax_Main;">0.1</span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.09em;"></span></span></span>元，但限定一条短信的内容在 <span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-2-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-4"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-5"><span class="mn" id="MathJax-Span-6" style="font-family:MathJax_Main;">70</span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.09em;"></span></span></span> 个字以内（包括 <span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-3-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-7"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-8"><span class="mn" id="MathJax-Span-9" style="font-family:MathJax_Main;">70</span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.09em;"></span></span></span> 个字）。如果一次所发送的短信超过了 <span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-4-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-10"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-11"><span class="mn" id="MathJax-Span-12" style="font-family:MathJax_Main;">70</span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.09em;"></span></span></span> 个字，则会按照每 <span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-5-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-13"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-14"><span class="mn" id="MathJax-Span-15" style="font-family:MathJax_Main;">70</span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.09em;"></span></span></span> 个字一条短信的限制把它分割成多条短信发送。假设已经知道某人当月所发送的短信的字数，试统计一下他当月短信的总资费。
<h2>输入格式</h2>

第一行是整数 <span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-6-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-16"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-17"><span class="mi" id="MathJax-Span-18" style="font-family:MathJax_Math;font-style:italic;">n</span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.076em;"></span></span></span>(<span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-7-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-19"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-20"><span class="mn" id="MathJax-Span-21" style="font-family:MathJax_Main;">1</span><span class="mo" id="MathJax-Span-22" style="font-family:MathJax_Main;">≤</span><span class="mi" id="MathJax-Span-23" style="font-family:MathJax_Math;font-style:italic;">n</span><span class="mo" id="MathJax-Span-24" style="font-family:MathJax_Main;">≤</span><span class="mn" id="MathJax-Span-25" style="font-family:MathJax_Main;">100</span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.235em;"></span></span></span>)，表示当月发送短信的总次数，接着 <span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-8-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-26"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-27"><span class="mi" id="MathJax-Span-28" style="font-family:MathJax_Math;font-style:italic;">n</span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.076em;"></span></span></span> 行每行一个整数(不超过 <span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-9-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-29"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-30"><span class="mn" id="MathJax-Span-31" style="font-family:MathJax_Main;">1000</span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.09em;"></span></span></span>)，表示每次短信的字数。
<h2>输出格式</h2>
