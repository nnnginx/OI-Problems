<h2>说明</h2>

马上要上课了，老师发现有一个学生没有来。 <br />
总共有<span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-1-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-1"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-2"><span class="mi" id="MathJax-Span-3" style="font-family:MathJax_Math;font-style:italic;">n</span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.076em;"></span></span></span>个学生参加，学生们的学号分别为<span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-2-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-4"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-5"><span class="mn" id="MathJax-Span-6" style="font-family:MathJax_Main;">1</span><span class="mo" id="MathJax-Span-7" style="font-family:MathJax_Main;">∼</span><span class="mi" id="MathJax-Span-8" style="font-family:MathJax_Math;font-style:italic;">n</span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.076em;"></span></span></span>。老师登记所有到位的学生的学号。老师希望能够帮她写一段代码找出来找到那个没来的学生。 <br />
<h2>输入格式</h2>

第—行是<span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-3-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-9"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-10"><span class="mi" id="MathJax-Span-11" style="font-family:MathJax_Math;font-style:italic;">n</span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.076em;"></span></span></span>&#44;为需要到位的学生个数。(<span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-4-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-12"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-13"><span class="mn" id="MathJax-Span-14" style="font-family:MathJax_Main;">1</span><span class="mo" id="MathJax-Span-15" style="font-family:MathJax_Main;">&lt;</span><span class="mi" id="MathJax-Span-16" style="font-family:MathJax_Math;font-style:italic;">n</span><span class="mo" id="MathJax-Span-17" style="font-family:MathJax_Main;">&lt;</span><span class="msubsup" id="MathJax-Span-18"><span><span><span class="mn" id="MathJax-Span-19" style="font-family:MathJax_Main;">10</span><span></span></span><span><span class="mn" id="MathJax-Span-20" style="font-size:70.7%;font-family:MathJax_Main;"><sup>6</sup></span><span></span></span></span></span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.112em;"></span></span></span>)。
	然后<span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-5-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-21"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-22"><span class="mi" id="MathJax-Span-23" style="font-family:MathJax_Math;font-style:italic;">n</span><span class="mo" id="MathJax-Span-24" style="font-family:MathJax_Main;">−</span><span class="mn" id="MathJax-Span-25" style="font-family:MathJax_Main;">1</span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.165em;"></span></span></span>行，每行一个整数，为到位的学生学号（不保证有序，但不会有一个学生出现两次)。 <br />
<h2>输出格式</h2>

一个数，为没有到位的学生的学号。
