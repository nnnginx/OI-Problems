<h2>说明</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">在学习加法时，我发现“进位”特别容易出错。你的任务是计算两个三位数在相加时需要多少次进位。你编制的程序应当可以连续处理多组数据，直到读到两个&nbsp;</span>0<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">（这是输入结束标记）。</span>
<h2>输入格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输入两个正整数&nbsp;</span>m<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&#44;</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">(&nbsp;</span>m<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&#44;</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">都是三位数)。</span>
<h2>输出格式</h2>

<pre><span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输出&nbsp;</span>m<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&#44;</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">，相加时需要进位多少次。</span> </pre>
<h2>样例</h2>
<pre><code class="language-input1">123 456
555 555
123 594
0 0</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">0
3
1</code></pre>
