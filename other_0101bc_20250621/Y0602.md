<h2>说明</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">育才小学四年级二班共有</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">人，其中参加语文兴趣小组的有</span>a<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">个人，参加数学兴趣小组的有</span>b<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">个人，两者都参加的有</span>c<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">个人，那么有多少人两个小组都没有参加？</span>
<h2>输入格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">在一行输入正整数</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;(</span>0&lt;n≤10000<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">)、&nbsp;</span>a<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">、&nbsp;</span>b<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">、&nbsp;</span>c<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;(</span>a&#44;b&#44;c≤n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">且&nbsp;</span>c&lt;min(a&#44;b)<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">)。</span>
<h2>输出格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输出有多少人两个兴趣小组都没有参加。</span>
<h2>样例</h2>
<pre><code class="language-input1">46 21 18 9</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">16</code></pre>
