<h2>说明</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;"> 
<p style="text-indent:2em;font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;">
	对于一个长度为&nbsp;n&nbsp;的序列 {a<sub>n</sub>} 来说，其众数被定义为出现次数最多的数。
</p>
<p style="text-indent:2em;font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;">
	现在给定一个长度为&nbsp;n 的序列，请帮忙求出它的众数是多少。
</p>
<p style="text-indent:2em;font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;">
	当然众数可能有多个，你只需要输出最小的一个就可以了。 <br />
<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;"></span>
</p>
</span>
<h2>输入格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;"><span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">第一行输入&nbsp;</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">，第二行输入&nbsp;</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;个数，</span>n&lt;10<sup>6</sup><span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&#44;</span>0≤a<sub>i</sub>≤1000<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">。</span></span><span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;"></span>
<h2>输出格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;"><span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输出众数。</span></span><span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;"></span>
<h2>样例</h2>
<pre><code class="language-input1">6
3 5 7 5 3 1</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">3</code></pre>
