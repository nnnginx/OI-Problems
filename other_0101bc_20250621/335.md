<h2>说明</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">晶晶的朋友贝贝约晶晶下周一起去看展览，但晶晶每周的&nbsp;</span>1<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">、</span>3<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">、</span>5<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;必须上课，请帮晶晶判断他能否接受贝贝的邀请，如果能输出"</span>YES<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">"；如果不能则输出"</span>NO<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">"。</span>
<h2>输入格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输入为一行，贝贝邀请晶晶去看展览的日期，用数字&nbsp;</span>1<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;到 </span>7<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;表示从星期一到星期日。</span>
<h2>输出格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输出为一行，如果晶晶可以接受贝贝的邀请，输出"</span>YES<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">"；否则，输出"</span>NO<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">"。</span>

<pre><code class="language-input1">2</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">YES</code></pre>
