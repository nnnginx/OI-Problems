<h2>说明</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">将</span>1<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&#44;</span>2<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&#44;…&#44;</span>9<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;共&nbsp;</span>9<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;个数分成三组，分别组成三个三位数，且使这三个三位数的比例是&nbsp;</span>A<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">:</span>B<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">:</span>C<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">，试求出所有满足条件的三个三位数，若无解，输出 “</span>No!!!<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">”。</span>
