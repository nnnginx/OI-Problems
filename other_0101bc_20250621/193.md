<h2>说明</h2>

输入一行单词序列，相邻单词之间由 <span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-1-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-1"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-2"><span class="mn" id="MathJax-Span-3" style="font-family:MathJax_Main;">1</span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.063em;"></span></span></span> 个或多个空格间隔，请对应地计算各个单词的长度。 <br />
注意，如果有标点符号（如连字符，逗号，句号），标点符号算作与之相连的词的一部分。没有被空格间隔开的符号串，都算作单词。 <br />
<h2>输入格式</h2>

一行单词序列，最少<span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-2-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-4"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-5"><span class="mn" id="MathJax-Span-6" style="font-family:MathJax_Main;">1</span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.063em;"></span></span></span>个单词，最多<span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-3-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-7"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-8"><span class="mn" id="MathJax-Span-9" style="font-family:MathJax_Main;">300</span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.09em;"></span></span></span>个单词，单词之间用至少<span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-4-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-10"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-11"><span class="mn" id="MathJax-Span-12" style="font-family:MathJax_Main;">1</span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.063em;"></span></span></span>个空格间隔。单词序列总长度不超过<span class="MathJax" id="MathJax-Element-5-fr<x>ame"><span class="math" id="MathJax-Span-13"><span style="font-size:125%;"><span><span class="mrow" id="MathJax-Span-14"><span class="mn" id="MathJax-Span-15" style="font-family:MathJax_Main;">1000</span></span><span></span></span></span><span style="vertical-align:-0.09em;"></span></span></span>。
<h2>输出格式</h2>

依次输出对应单词的长度，之间以逗号间隔。
<h2>样例</h2>
<pre><code class="language-input1">She was born in 1990-01-02  and  from Beijing city.</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">3,3,4,2,10,3,4,7,5</code></pre>
