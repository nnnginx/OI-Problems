<h2>说明</h2>

<p class="p1" style="font-size:14px;font-family:&quot;">
	<span class="s1" style="line-height:normal;font-family:&quot;">把</span>3.14159 <span class="s1" style="line-height:normal;font-family:&quot;">四舍五人保留</span>n<span class="s1" style="line-height:normal;font-family:&quot;">位小数</span>(1≤n≤5)<span class="s1" style="line-height:normal;font-family:&quot;">。如</span>n=1<span class="s1" style="line-height:normal;font-family:&quot;">时输出</span>3.1；n=4 <span class="s1" style="line-height:normal;font-family:&quot;">时输出</span> 3.1416<span class="s1" style="line-height:normal;font-family:&quot;">。</span> 
</p>
<h2>输入格式</h2>

保留小数的位数n
<h2>输出格式</h2>

3.14159保留n位后的小数
<h2>样例</h2>
<pre><code class="language-input1">4</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">3.1416</code></pre>
