<h2>说明</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">楼梯有</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">(</span>71&gt;n&gt;0<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">)阶台阶&#44;上楼时可以一步上</span>1<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">阶&#44;也可以一步上</span>2<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">阶&#44;也可以一步上</span>3<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">阶，编程计算共有多少种不同的走法。</span>
<h2>输入格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输入的每一行包括一组测试数据，即为台阶数</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">。最后一行为</span>0<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">，表示测试结束。</span>
<h2>输出格式</h2>

每一行输出对应一行输入的结果，即为走法的数目。
