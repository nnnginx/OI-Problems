<h2>说明</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">一个正整数如果能表示成两个正整数的平方差，则称这个数为一个“智慧数”，比如</span>16<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">就等于</span>5<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">的平方减去</span>3<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">的平方，所以</span>16<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">就是一个智慧数，从</span>1<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">开始的自然数列中，将“智慧数"从小到大编号为</span>1<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&#44;</span>2<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&#44;</span>3<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&#44;...&#44;</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">。现输入一个正整数</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">，输出第</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">个“智慧数”。</span>
<h2>输入格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输入仅包含一个正整数</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;(</span>1≤n≤100<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">)。</span>
<h2>输出格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输出仅包含一个正整数，表示编号为</span>n<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">的“智慧数”。</span>
<h2>样例</h2>
<pre><code class="language-input1">3</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">7</code></pre>
