<h2>说明</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">歌手大奖赛上</span>6<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">名评委给一位参赛者打分，</span>6<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">个人打分的平均分为 a&nbsp;</span><span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">分；如果去掉一个最高分，这名参赛者的平均分为</span>&nbsp;b&nbsp;<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">分；如果去掉一个最低分，这名参赛者的平均分为</span>&nbsp;c&nbsp;<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">分；如果去掉一个最高分和一个最低分，这名参赛者的平均是多少？</span>

<h2>输入格式</h2>

<p>
	三个实数a，b，c
</p>
<h2>输出格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">使用</span>%5.2f<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">按实数格式输出，保留</span>2<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">位小数。</span>

<h2>样例</h2>
<pre><code class="language-input1">9.6 9.4 9.8</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">9.60</code></pre>
