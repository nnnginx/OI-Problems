<h2>说明</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">给定&nbsp;</span>N<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&nbsp;个正整数，请统计奇数和偶数各有多少个。</span>
<h2>输入格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输入第一行给出一个正整&nbsp;</span>N<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">(</span>N≤1000<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">)；第&nbsp;</span>2<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">行到第&nbsp;</span>N+1<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">行&#44;每行一个整数。</span>
<h2>输出格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">在一行中先后输出奇数的个数、偶数的个数。中间以&nbsp;</span>1<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">个空格分隔。</span>
<h2>样例</h2>
<pre><code class="language-input1">3
88
74
101</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">1 2</code></pre>
