<h2>说明</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">对于多项式</span>f(x)=ax<sup>3</sup>+bx<sup>2</sup>+cx+d<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">和给定的</span>a<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&#44;</span>b<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&#44;</span>c<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&#44;</span>d<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">&#44;</span>x<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">，计算</span>f(x)<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">的值，保留到小数点后</span>7<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">位。</span>
<h2>输入格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输入仅一行，包含</span>5<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">个实数，分别是</span>x<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">，及参数</span>a<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">、</span>b<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">、</span>c<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">、</span>d<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">的值，每个数都是绝对值不超过</span>100<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">的双精度浮点数。数与数之间以一个空格分开。</span>
<h2>输出格式</h2>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">输出一个实数，即</span>f(x)<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">的值，保留到小数点后</span>7<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:medium;background-color:#FCFCFC;">位。</span>
