<h2>Description</h2><p>Peter has n cigarettes. He smokes them one by one keeping all the butts. Out of k &gt; 1 butts he can roll a new cigarette. 
</p>How many cigarettes can Peter have? 
<h2>Input</h2><p>Input is a sequence of lines. Each line contains two integer numbers giving the values of n and k. </p><h2>Output</h2><p>For each line of input, output one integer number on a separate line giving the maximum number of cigarettes that Peter can have.</p><pre><code class="language-input1">4 3
10 3
100 5
</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">5
14
124
