## 输入格式

<p class="NOI0" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><font size="3"><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">第一行一个整数</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">T</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">，表示数据组数。</span></font></p> 
<p class="NOI0" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><font size="3"><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">以下每一行两个数</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">L</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">、</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">R</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">（保证区间合法），代表询问</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">[L, R]</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">。</span></font></p> 
<p></p>

## 输出格式

<p class="NOI0" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><font size="3"><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">输出</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">T</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">行，每行一个数，表示在这个区间内小</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">D</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">喜欢的数出现了多少次。</span></font></p> 
<p class="NOI0" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><font size="3">你的输出当且仅当和标准输出一样才能得该测试点满分。</font></span></p> 
<p class="NOI1" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt"></p>

```input1
3
1 5
3 9
8 8
```
```output1
2
2
0
```
