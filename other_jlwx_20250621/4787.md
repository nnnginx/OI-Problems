<h2>说明</h2>
<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:15px;background-color:#F5F5F5;">从键盘输入整数L，统计出边长为整数，周长为L的不等边三角形的个数</span>

<span style="font-family:&quot;font-size:15px;background-color:#F5F5F5;">三角形个数</span>
<pre><code class="language-input1">15</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">6</code></pre>
