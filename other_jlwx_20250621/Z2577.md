## 题目描述
<!--StartFragment -->
<div>
 给你一个正整数A,B(a,b<=10^500).要你通过以下三种操作将A变成B。操作分别是
 <br>
  a=a+1
 <br>
  a=a-1
 <br>
  a=2*a
 <br>
  输出最小需要的操作次数
</div>

## 输入格式

<p><span style="font-size: medium">There are several test cases, ended by “0 0”.<br>
   For each test case, there are only one line containing two numbers separated by a blank, N and M, the original numbers of peaches and the numbers of peaches left(0<N,M<10^500).There is no leading zero.<br></span></p>

## 输出格式

<div class="panel_content">
 <span style="font-size: medium">For each test case, you should output just a number K indicating the minimum time (in minutes) Lily needed to finish her illusion magic.</span>
</div>

```input1
5 2
1 99
86 32
0 0
```
```output1
2
98
12
```
