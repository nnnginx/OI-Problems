## 题目描述

```input1
4 6
4 3
3 5
2 5
2 4
```
```output1
9
```
## 数据范围与约定

<p>An available assignment is the first two pairs are assigned into the first part and the last two pairs are assigned into the second part. Then B1 > A3, B1 > A4, B2 > A3, B2 > A4, max{A1, A2}+max{A3, A4} ≤ 6, and minimum max{B1+B2, B3+B4}=9.</p>
