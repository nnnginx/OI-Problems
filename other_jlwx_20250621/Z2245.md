## 题目描述

<p class="NOI0" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><font size="3"><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">你的公司接到了一批订单。订单要求你的公司提供</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">n</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">类产品，产品被编号为</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">1~n</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">，其中第</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">i</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">类产品共需要</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">C<sub>i</sub></font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">件。公司共有</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">m</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">名员工，员工被编号为</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">1~m</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">员工能够制造的产品种类有所区别。一件产品必须完整地由一名员工制造，不可以由某名员工制造一部分配件后，再转交给另外一名员工继续进行制造。</span></font></p>
<p class="NOI0" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><font size="3"><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">我们用一个由</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">0</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">和</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">1</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">组成的</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">m*n</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">的矩阵</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">A</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">来描述每名员工能够制造哪些产品。矩阵的行和列分别被编号为</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">1~m</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">和</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">1~n</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">，</span><font face="Times New Roman"><span lang="EN-US">A</span><span lang="EN-US"><sub>i,j</sub></span></font><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">为</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">1</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">表示员工</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">i</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">能够制造产品</span><span lang="EN-US"><span style="position: relative; top: 3pt; mso-text-raise: -3.0pt"><font face="Times New Roman">j</font></span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">，为</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">0</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">表示员工</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">i</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">不能制造产品</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">j</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">。</span></font></p>
<p class="NOI0" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><font size="3">如果公司分配了过多工作给一名员工，这名员工会变得不高兴。我们用愤怒值来描述某名员工的心情状态。愤怒值越高，表示这名员工心情越不爽，愤怒值越低，表示这名员工心情越愉快。员工的愤怒值与他被安排制造的产品数量存在某函数关系，鉴于员工们的承受能力不同，不同员工之间的函数关系也是有所区别的。</font></span></p>
<p class="NOI0" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><font size="3"><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">对于员工</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">i</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">，他的愤怒值与产品数量之间的函数是一个</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">S<sub>i</sub>+1</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">段的分段函数。当他制造第</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">1~T<sub>i,1</sub></font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">件产品时，每件产品会使他的愤怒值增加</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">W<sub>i,1</sub></font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">，当他制造第</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">T<sub>i,1</sub>+1~T<sub>i,2</sub></font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">件产品时，每件产品会使他的愤怒值增加</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">W<sub>i,2</sub></font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">……为描述方便，设</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">T<sub>i,0</sub>=0,T<sub>i,s<span style="position: relative; top: 2pt; mso-text-raise: -2.0pt">i+1</span></sub><span style="position: relative; top: 2pt; mso-text-raise: -2.0pt">=</span></font></span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; position: relative; top: 2pt; mso-text-raise: -2.0pt">+</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; position: relative; top: 2pt; mso-text-raise: -2.0pt">∞</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">，那么当他制造第</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">T<sub>i,j-1</sub>+1~T<sub>i,j</sub></font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">件产品时，每件产品会使他的愤怒值增加</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">W<sub>i,j</sub></font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">，</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman"> 1</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体">≤</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">j</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体">≤<span lang="EN-US">S<sub>i</sub>+1</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">。</span></font></p>
<p class="NOI0" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><font size="3"><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">你的任务是制定出一个产品的分配方案，使得订单条件被满足，并且所有员工的愤怒值之和最小。由于我们并不想使用</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">Special Judge</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">，也为了使选手有更多的时间研究其他两道题目，你只需要输出最小的愤怒值之和就可以了。</span></font></p>

## 输入格式

<p class="NOI0" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><font size="3"><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">第一行包含两个正整数</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">m</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">和</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">n</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">，分别表示员工数量和产品的种类数；</span></font></p>
<p class="NOI0" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><font size="3"><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">第二行包含</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">n </font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">个正整数，第</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">i</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">个正整数为</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">C<sub>i</sub></font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">；</span></font></p>
<p class="NOI0" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><font size="3"><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">以下</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">m</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">行每行</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">n </font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">个整数描述矩阵</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">A</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">；</span></font></p>
<p class="NOI0" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><font size="3"><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">下面</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">m</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">个部分，第</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">i</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">部分描述员工</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">i</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">的愤怒值与产品数量的函数关系。每一部分由三行组成：第一行为一个非负整数</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">S<sub>i</sub></font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">，第二行包含</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">S<sub>i</sub></font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">个正整数，其中第</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">j</font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">个正整数为</span><span lang="EN-US"><font face="Times New Roman">T<sub>i,j</sub></font></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; ## 输出格式

<p class="NOI0" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt"><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'"><font size="3">仅输出一个整数，表示最小的愤怒值之和。</font></span></p>
<p class="NOI" style="margin: 13pt 0cm"></p>

```input1
2 3
2 2 2
1 1 0
0 0 1
1
2
1 10
1
2
1 6
```
```output1
24
```
