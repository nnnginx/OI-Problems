<h2>说明</h2>
<p class="p0">
	<span style="font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:'宋体';">输入两个字符串，比较两字符串的长度大小关系。</span>
</p>
<h2>输入格式</h2>
<p class="p0">
	<span style="font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:'宋体';">输入第一行表示测试用例的个数<span>m</span><span>，接下来</span><span>m</span><span>行每行两个字符串</span><span>A</span><span>和</span><span>B</span><span>，字符串长度不超过</span><span>50</span><span>。</span></span>
</p>
<h2>输出格式</h2>
<p class="p0">
	<span style="font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:'宋体';">输出<span>m</span><span>行。若两字符串长度相等则输出</span><span>A&nbsp;is&nbsp;equal&nbsp;long&nbsp;to&nbsp;B</span><span>；若</span><span>A</span><span>比</span><span>B</span><span>长，则输出</span><span>A&nbsp;is&nbsp;longer&nbsp;than&nbsp;B</span><span>；否则输出</span><span>A&nbsp;is&nbsp;shorter&nbsp;than&nbsp;B</span><span>。</span></span><span style="font-size:10.5000pt;font-family:'宋体';"></span>
</p>
<pre><code class="language-input1">2
abc xy
bbb ccc</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">abc is longer than xy
bbb is equal long to ccc</code></pre>
