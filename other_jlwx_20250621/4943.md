<h2>说明</h2>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:15.75pt;text-indent:-15.75pt;">
	<span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">设有一个数组&nbsp;</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">A:ARRAY[0..N-1] OF INTEGER</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">；数组中存放的元素为</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">0</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">～</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">N-1</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">之间的整数，且</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">A[i]</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">≠</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">A[j]</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">（当</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">i</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">≠</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">j</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">时）。</span><span></span> 
</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">
	<span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">例如：</span> 
</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">
	<span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;N=6</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">时，有：</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">A=(</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">4,</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">3,</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">0,</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">5,</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">1,</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">2)</span><span></span> 
</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">
	<span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">此时，数组</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">A</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">的编码定义如下：</span><span></span> 
</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">
	<span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A[0]</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">的编码为</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">0</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">；</span><span></span> 
</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">
	<span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A[i]</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">的编码为：在</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">A[0]</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">，</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">A[1]</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">，……</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">A[i-1]</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">中比</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">A[i]</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">的值小的个数（</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">i=1</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">，</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">2</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">……</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">N-1</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">）</span><span></span> 
</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">
	<span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">∴上面数组</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">A</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">的编码为：</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">B=(</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">0,</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">0,</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">0,</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">3,</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">1,</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">2)</span> 
</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">
	<span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';"> </span> 
</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">
	<b><span style="font-family:Microsoft YaHei;">程序要求解决以下问题：</span><span></span></b> 
</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:15.75pt;text-indent:5.25pt;">
	<span style="font-family:Microsoft YaHei;">①&nbsp;&nbsp;
给出数组</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">A</span><span style="font-family:Microsoft YaHei;">后，求出其编码；</span><span></span> 
</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:15.75pt;text-indent:5.25pt;">
	<span style="font-family:Microsoft YaHei;">②&nbsp;&nbsp;
给出数组</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">A</span><span style="font-family:Microsoft YaHei;">的编码后，求出</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">A</span><span style="font-family:Microsoft YaHei;">中的原数据。</span> 
</p>
<p>
	<br />
</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:15.75pt;text-indent:5.25pt;">
	<br />
</p>
<h2>输入格式</h2>
<p>
	每个测试文件只包含一组测试数据，每组输入包含三行。
</p>
<p>
	第一行输入整数N；
</p>
<p>
	第二行输入有两种可能：
</p>
<p>
	例如：
</p>
<p>
	A=(4,3,0,5,1,2)
</p>
<p>
	或
</p>
<p>
	B=(0,0,0,3,1,2)
</p>
<p>
	其中输入中的逗号和括号都是英文状态下的。
</p>
<h2>输出格式</h2>
<p>
	当输入的是A=(...)，则输出其编码。
</p>
<p>
	当输入的是B=(...)，则输出A中的原数据。
</p>
<p>
	输出数据的格式和输入数据的格式是一样的。
</p>
<pre><code class="language-input1">6
A=(4,3,0,5,1,2)
</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">B=(0,0,0,3,1,2)
</code></pre>
