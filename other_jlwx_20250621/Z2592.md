## 题目描述

<p><span style="font-size: larger"><span class="Apple-style-span" style="word-spacing: 0px; font: medium Arial,Microsoft Yahei,Simsun,sans-serif; text-transform: none; color: rgb(0,0,0); text-indent: 0px; white-space: normal; letter-spacing: normal; border-collapse: separate; orphans: 2; widows: 2; webkit-border-horizontal-spacing: 0px; webkit-border-vertical-spacing: 0px; webkit-text-decorations-in-effect: none; webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px"><span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: monospace">After taking a modern art class, Farmer John has become interested in finding geometric patterns in everything around his farm. He carefully plots the locations of his N cows (2 <= N <= 1000), each one occupying a distinct point in the 2D plane, and he wonders how many different lines of symmetry exist for this set of points. A line of symmetry, of course, is a line across which the points on both sides are mirror images of each-other. Please help FJ answer this most pressing geometric question. </span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: larger">上过现代艺术课后，FJ开始感兴趣于在他农场中的几何图样。他计划将奶牛放置在二维平面上的N个互不相同的点(1<=N<=1000),他希望找出这个点集有多少条对称轴。他急切地需要你帮忙解决这个几何问题。<br></span></p>

## 输入格式

<p><span style="font-size: medium"><span class="Apple-style-span" style="word-spacing: 0px; font: medium Arial,Microsoft Yahei,Simsun,sans-serif; text-transform: none; color: rgb(0,0,0); text-indent: 0px; white-space: normal; letter-spacing: normal; border-collapse: separate; orphans: 2; widows: 2; webkit-border-horizontal-spacing: 0px; webkit-border-vertical-spacing: 0px; webkit-text-decorations-in-effect: none; webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px"><span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: monospace">* Line 1: The single integer N. </span></span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium"><span class="Apple-style-span" style="word-spacing: 0px; font: medium Arial,Microsoft Yahei,Simsun,sans-serif; text-transform: none; color: rgb(0,0,0); text-indent: 0px; white-space: normal; letter-spacing: normal; border-collapse: separate; orphans: 2; widows: 2; webkit-border-horizontal-spacing: 0px; webkit-border-vertical-spacing: 0px; webkit-text-decorations-in-effect: none; webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px"><span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: monospace">* Lines 2..1+N: Line i+1 contains two space-separated integers representing the x and y coordinates of the ith cow (-10,000 <= x,y <= 10,000). </span></span></span></p>
<p></p>

## 输出格式

<p><span style="font-size: medium"><span class="Apple-style-span" style="word-spacing: 0px; font: medium Arial,Microsoft Yahei,Simsun,sans-serif; text-transform: none; color: rgb(0,0,0); text-indent: 0px; white-space: normal; letter-spacing: normal; border-collapse: separate; orphans: 2; widows: 2; webkit-border-horizontal-spacing: 0px; webkit-border-vertical-spacing: 0px; webkit-text-decorations-in-effect: none; webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px"><span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: monospace">* Line 1: The number of different lines of symmetry of the point set. </span></span></span></p>

```input1
4
0 0
0 1
1 0
1 1
```
```output1
4
```
