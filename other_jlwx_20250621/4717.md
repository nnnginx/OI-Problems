<h2>输出格式</h2>
<span style="font-family:'Segoe UI', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif;font-size:15px;background-color:#F5F5F5;">m个整数（空格隔开）</span>
<pre><code class="language-input1">3
1 7 5</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">5 7 1</code></pre>
