<h2>说明</h2>
<p>
	<span style="font-family:Arial;">FatMouse prepared M pounds of cat food, ready to trade with the cats guarding the warehouse containing his favorite food, JavaBean.<br />
The warehouse has N rooms. The i-th room contains J[i] pounds of JavaBeans and requires F[i] pounds of cat food. FatMouse does not have to trade for all the JavaBeans in the room, instead, he may get J[i]* a% pounds of JavaBeans if he pays F[i]* a% pounds of cat food. Here a is a real number. Now he is assigning this homework to you: tell him the maximum amount of JavaBeans he can obtain. <br />
</span> 
</p>
<p>
	<br />
</p>
<h2>输入格式</h2>
<p>
	<span style="font-family:Arial;">The input consists of multiple test cases. Each test case begins with a line containing two non-negative integers M and N. Then N lines follow, each contains two non-negative integers J[i] and F[i] respectively. The last test case is followed by two -1's. All integers are not greater than 1000. </span> 
</p>
<p>
	<br />
</p>
<h2>输出格式</h2>
<p>
	<span style="font-family:Arial;">For each test case, print in a single line a real number accurate up to 3 decimal places, which is the maximum amount of JavaBeans that FatMouse can obtain. </span> 
</p>
<p>
	<br />
</p>
<pre><code class="language-input1">4 2
4 7
1 3
5 5
4 8
3 8
1 2
2 5
2 4
-1 -1</code></pre><pre><code class="language-output1">2.286
2.500
</code></pre>
