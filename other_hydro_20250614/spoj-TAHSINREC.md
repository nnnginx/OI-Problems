<p><span id="docs-internal-guid-40e17f69-7fff-192e-ddd2-dbbd0eebcb0e"> </span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.2; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;" dir="ltr"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; background-color: transparent; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">While going through her friend¡¯s secret diary, one-day Poga came upon a function. <p style="line-height: 1.2; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt; text-align: center;" dir="ltr">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.2; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt; text-align: center;" dir="ltr"><span style="background-color: transparent; font-family: Arial; font-size: 12pt; white-space: pre-wrap;">f(n, k) - f(n-1,k) = f(n-1,k-1),</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.2; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt; text-align: center;" dir="ltr"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial; background-color: transparent; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">f(n, 0) = 1</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.2; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt; text-align: center;" dir="ltr"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial; background-color: transparent; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">and </span><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial; background-color: transparent; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">f(n,k) = 0 </span><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial; background-color: transparent; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">when n &nbsp;&lt; k</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; background-color: transparent; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">Seeing how this is a recursive function, Poga got very scared. As the answer can become very big, you should print the answer modulo </span><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">M</span><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial; background-color: transparent; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">.</span></p>

<h3>Input</h3>
<p><span id="docs-internal-guid-c8bb351e-7fff-4af5-8db6-6c6059c4d042"> </span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.5054545454545452; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 10pt; text-align: justify;" dir="ltr"><span style="font-size: 13pt; color: #00000a; background-color: transparent; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;"><span style="font-size: small;">Input starts with an integer <strong>T</strong>, denoting the number of test cases.</span></span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.5054545454545452; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 10pt; text-align: justify;" dir="ltr"><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="background-color: transparent; color: #00000a; white-space: pre-wrap;">Then each of the </span>next<span style="background-color: transparent; color: #00000a; white-space: pre-wrap;"> T lines contains three integers </span><strong>N</strong><span style="background-color: transparent; color: #00000a; white-space: pre-wrap;">, </span><strong>K</strong><span style="background-color: transparent; color: #00000a; white-space: pre-wrap;">,&nbsp;</span>and<span style="background-color: transparent; color: #00000a; white-space: pre-wrap;"> </span><strong>M</strong><span style="background-color: transparent; color: #00000a; white-space: pre-wrap;">.</span></span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.5054545454545452; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 10pt; text-align: justify;" dir="ltr"><span style="font-size: 13pt; font-family: Arial; color: #00000a; background-color: transparent; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;"> </span><span style="font-size: 13pt; font-family: Arial; font-weight: 700; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;"><br></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-weight: 700; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">Constraints:</span><span style="font-weight: 700; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;"><br></span><span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">1 &lt;= T &lt;= 100 </span></span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.5054545454545452; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 10pt; text-align: justify;" dir="ltr"><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">1 &lt;= N &lt;= 10</span><span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: super; white-space: pre-wrap;">5</span></span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.5054545454545452; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 10pt; text-align: justify;" dir="ltr"><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">0 &lt;= K &lt;= 10</span><span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: super; white-space: pre-wrap;">5</span></span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.5054545454545452; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 10pt; text-align: justify;" dir="ltr"><span style="font-size: small;"><span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">1 &lt;= M &lt;= 10</span><span style="font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: super; white-space: pre-wrap;">12</span></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: small;">For each test case, print the answer, value of <em>f(<strong>N</strong>, <strong>K</strong>)</em> modulo <strong>M</strong>.</span></p>
