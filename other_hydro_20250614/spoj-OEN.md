<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: small;"><strong><span style="font-size: medium;">Description:</span></strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium;">Suppose you have a number of three digits, which is <strong>P</strong>. Add those three digits and store in <strong>X</strong>. Then subtract <strong>X</strong> from the actual number <strong>P</strong>. After Subtraction, now the resulting number is <strong>N</strong>. In this problem, you are given sum of last two digits of N. Your task is to find the first digit of <strong>N.</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium;">For example, suppose <strong>P</strong> = 123, <strong>X</strong> = 1+2+3 = 6, <strong>N</strong> = P - X =123 - 6 = 117 . Sum of last two digits is 8 . So, in this case, first digit is 1</span></p>
<h3><span style="font-size: medium;">Input</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-size: medium;">Input starts with an integer T (1&lt;=1000000), denoting the number of test cases. Each case contains sum of last two digits of N (0 ¡Ü N ¡Ü 18).</span></p>
<h3><span style="font-size: medium;">Output</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-size: medium;">For each test case, output one line containing "Case <strong>x</strong>: <strong>M</strong>¡°, where <strong>x</strong> is the test case number,&nbsp; <strong>M</strong> is the first digit of <strong>N</strong>.</span></p>
<h3><span style="font-size: medium;">Example</span></h3>
<pre><span style="font-size: medium;"><strong>Input:</strong>
</span><p><span style="font-size: medium;">2</span></p><p><span style="font-size: medium;">8</span></p><p><span style="font-size: medium;">13</span></p>
<span style="font-size: medium;"><strong>Output:</strong>
</span><p><span style="font-size: medium;">Case 1: 1</span></p><p><span style="font-size: medium;">Case 2: 5</span></p></pre>
