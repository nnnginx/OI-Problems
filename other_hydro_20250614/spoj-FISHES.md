<p>Marlin engaged Dory, last April and they decided to marry in next November. Unfortunately, Dory&nbsp; is not like normal girls who their dowry is money, rings or furniture! She has many pictures of fishes and she wants a program that draws boxes around the fishes in the images. Time is passing and Marlin¡¯s program just process the image and output some data that could be used to find the correct locations of these boxes. Please help him to save his marriage dream.</p>
<p>Marlin outputs for every image:</p>
<ul>
<li>2 integers H and K.</li>
<li>A 2D Matrix M of integer values from 1 to K.</li>
<li>T integer vectors V<sub>i</sub>, each of length K. Each vector is coupled with an integer value X<sub>i</sub>.</li>
</ul>
<p>A Score Evaluator function is used to calculate the score of any box in the image, such that the higher box score, the more confidence in the box to tightly contain a fish. Hence, Marlin targets the box with the highest score that contains a fish.</p>
<p>Given 4 corners of box B, the function works as following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Construct vector V that has the frequencies of the values in M corresponding to B.</li>
<li>Calculate the score as:</li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong><span style="font-size: medium;">Score = H + SUM{i=1 to T}[X<sub>i</sub> * (V.V<sub>i</sub>)]</span></strong></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">where (a.b) means the dot product.</p>
