<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px 0px 10px;"><span style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">For each query, Pavel should do the following:</span></p>
<ol style="box-sizing: border-box; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 10px;">
<li style="box-sizing: border-box;">
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px 0px 10px;"><span style="font-family: tahoma, arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">First, he has to insert the numbers at index L, L+1, L+2,¡­¡­,R of the given array into a multi-set.
