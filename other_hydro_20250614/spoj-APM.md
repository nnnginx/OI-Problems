<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px 0px 10px; color: #555555; font-family: &quot;Open Sans&quot;, &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; text-align: center;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px 0px 10px; color: #555555; font-family: &quot;Open Sans&quot;, &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 14px; text-align: left;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: 700;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-size: medium;">Description:</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium;">Mr. Abul is a big fan of <strong>prime numbers</strong>. As a fan of prime numbers, he wants to know whether a prime number <strong>P</strong> can be expressed as <strong>Difference of two Squared Number</strong>&nbsp;or not?.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium;">In other words you have to calculate two natural numbers <strong>X</strong> and <strong>Y</strong> where <strong>P</strong> = <strong>X^2</strong> - <strong>Y^2</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium;">For example, prime number <strong>P</strong> = 5 can be expressed as 3^2 - 2^2 = 9 - 4 = 5, here X = 3 and Y = 2.</span></p>
<h3><span style="font-size: medium;">Input</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-size: medium;">Input starts with an integer <strong>T</strong>&lt;=100, denoting the number of test cases.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium;">Each case contains an integer <strong>N</strong>(2 ¡Ü N ¡Ü 10^18) denoting a prime number.</span></p>
<h3><span style="font-size: medium;">Output</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-size: medium;">For each case of input, print X and Y separated by a space if it is possible to express as Difference of two Squared Number. Otherwise, print -1.</span></p>
<h3><span style="font-size: medium;">Example</span></h3>
<pre><span style="font-size: medium;"><strong>Input:</strong>
</span><p><span style="font-size: medium;">3</span></p><p><span style="font-size: medium;">2</span></p><p><span style="font-size: medium;">3</span></p><p><span style="font-size: medium;">5</span></p><span style="font-size: medium;"><strong>Output:</strong>
</span><p><span style="font-size: medium;">-1</span></p><p><span style="font-size: medium;">2 1</span></p><p><span style="font-size: medium;">3 2</span></p></pre>
