<p>Gitu is the smartest economist in the city. He has been kidnapped by Ankur and Vaibhav. They want him&nbsp;</p>
<p>to make profit by Arbitrage.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Arbitrage, roughly, is the process of taking advantage of price differences in various economies/markets&nbsp;</p>
<p>to earn profit.</p>
<p>So Ankur and Vaibhav have some units of each of the N valuables items to be sold in exactly N different&nbsp;</p>
<p>markets in exchange of some money. Now they want Gitu to sell the items intelligently and maximize&nbsp;</p>
<p>the total money earned. However, there is one condition. If Gitu sells some units of a particular item in&nbsp;</p>
<p>a particular market, he cannot sell further units of that item in any other market and he cannot sell any&nbsp;</p>
<p>other item¡¯s units in this market.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Ankur and Vaibhav have allowed Gitu to talk to you and only you. Can you help Gitu to strategize the sell&nbsp;</p>
<p>to make maximum earning?</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>Input</h3>
<p>First line consists of T, the number of test cases.</p>
<p>Each test case starts with an integer N, the different items to be sold and the different markets present.</p>
<p>Next line contains N space separated integers, the units of each of the items Ankur and Vaibhav have.</p>
<p>Each of the next N lines consists of N space separated integers. jth integer in ith line signifies the money&nbsp;</p>
<p>earned after selling one unit of jth item in ith market.</p>
<h3>Output</h3>
<p>Output T lines corresponding to each test case, the maximum money that can be earned by following&nbsp;</p>
<p>the process stated above.</p>
