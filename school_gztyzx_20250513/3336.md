<h2>说明</h2>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:15.75pt;text-indent:-15.75pt;">
	<span style="font-family:Microsoft YaHei;">设在一排上有</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">N</span><span style="font-family:Microsoft YaHei;">个格子（</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">N</span><span style="font-family:Microsoft YaHei;">≤</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">20</span><span style="font-family:Microsoft YaHei;">），若在格子中放置有不同颜色的灯，每种灯的个数记为</span><span><span style="font-family:Microsoft YaHei;">N</span><sub><span style="font-family:Microsoft YaHei;">1</span></sub></span><span style="font-family:Microsoft YaHei;">，</span><span><span style="font-family:Microsoft YaHei;">N</span><sub><span style="font-family:Microsoft YaHei;">2</span></sub></span><span style="font-family:Microsoft YaHei;">，……</span><span><span style="font-family:Microsoft YaHei;">N</span><sub><span style="font-family:Microsoft YaHei;">k</span></sub></span><span style="font-family:Microsoft YaHei;">（</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">k</span><span style="font-family:Microsoft YaHei;">表示不同颜色灯的个数）。</span><span></span> 
</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">
	<span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">&nbsp;&nbsp; </span><b><span style="font-family:Microsoft YaHei;">放灯时要遵守下列规则：</span><span></span></b> 
</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:31.5pt;text-indent:-10.5pt;">
	<span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">①</span><span style="font-family:Microsoft YaHei;">同一种颜色的灯不能分开；</span><span></span> 
</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:31.5pt;text-indent:-10.5pt;">
	<span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">②</span><span style="font-family:Microsoft YaHei;">不同颜色的灯之间至少要有一个空位置。</span><span></span> 
</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">
	<span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">&nbsp; &nbsp;</span><span style="font-family:Microsoft YaHei;">例如：</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">N=8</span><span style="font-family:Microsoft YaHei;">（格子数）</span><span></span> 
</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">
	<span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">&nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;R=2</span><span style="font-family:Microsoft YaHei;">（红灯数）</span><span></span> 
</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">
	<span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;B=3</span><span style="font-family:Microsoft YaHei;">（蓝灯数）</span><span></span> 
</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">
	<span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">&nbsp; &nbsp;</span><span style="font-family:Microsoft YaHei;">放置的方法有：</span><span></span> 
</p>

<h2>输入格式</h2>

<p class="MsoNormal">
	<span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">数据输入的方式为：</span> 
</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:36pt;">
	<span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">N</span> 
</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:36pt;">
	<span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">P1</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">（颜色，为一个字母）</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">&nbsp;N1</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">（灯的数量）</span> 
</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:36pt;">
	<span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">P2&nbsp;N2</span> 
</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:36pt;">
	<span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">……</span> 
</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left:36pt;">
	<span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">Q</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">（结束标记，</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">Q</span><span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">本身不是灯的颜色）</span>

<p class="MsoNormal">
	<span style="font-family:'Microsoft YaHei';">&nbsp;颜色和灯的数量之间由一个空格分隔。</span> 
</p>
<h2>输出格式</h2>

输出排列总数。

<pre><code class="language-input1">8
